About Me

Hi! my name is Kevin Coscarelli, I've been working as a Front-end/Full-stack engineer for the past nine years. I've worked for two major consulting companies in Argentina where I got the opportunity to work for a variety of clients such as Coca Cola, Disney, Sophos, Delphix, Thomas Cook and Reed among others and upon relocating to Denmark I worked at Lenus and later at Ageras Group. I've worked in both small and large international teams where I learned to implement agile workflow methodologies and to articulate different tech stacks, the most important of them being AngularJS, Angular, React, Backbone, Node, GraphQL, React-Native, Laravel, Docker and AWS. During all of these years curiosity has been my main drive, both when I landed my first job and barely knew anything about software development, and today, when I see myself as a more seasoned developer but still remain eager to learn new things. I've grown as a developer to a point where I can not only make a positive contribution to everyday teamwork, but also, due to the many challenges I faced and the experience I got from them, I can help others in their work as well as contribute in product design and software architecture.
